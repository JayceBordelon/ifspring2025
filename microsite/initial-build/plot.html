The Legend Unfolds

A century of silence. A kingdom in ruins. A hero reborn.

Set at the end of the Zelda timeline, Link awakens from a 100-year slumber with no memory of his past. He learns from the spirit of King Rhoam that Calamity Ganon has been sealed within Hyrule Castle, but is growing stronger.

Reclaim the Divine Beasts

Link must recover control of four ancient mechanical constructs—Divine Beasts—each once piloted by a Champion of Hyrule. These Beasts hold the key to weakening Calamity Ganon’s hold.

Free the Spirits of the Champions

Inside the Divine Beasts, Link confronts corrupted manifestations of Ganon. By defeating them, he frees the spirits of the fallen Champions, reclaiming their strength.

Face Calamity Ganon

With the Divine Beasts and the Master Sword, Link ventures into Hyrule Castle for a final confrontation. There, he must defeat Calamity Ganon before the kingdom is consumed.

Memories of Zelda

Throughout his journey, Link slowly regains memories of Princess Zelda, their past, and the ancient bond that connects them. Her power and sacrifice remain the kingdom's final hope.
