Gameplay

Venture into a wild, beautiful world. How you survive is up to you.

Exploration

Traverse diverse landscapes such as forests, mountains, and ruins. The open world rewards curiosity and grants freedom like never before in the series.

Combat

Engage enemies with weapons that degrade over time, requiring creative strategy and resourcefulness to survive dynamic encounters.

Puzzles and Shrines

Discover over 100 shrines, each a unique puzzle that tests your mind. Completing them earns Spirit Orbs that enhance health or stamina.

Survival Mechanics

Manage weather, temperature, and cooking. Meals and elixirs offer healing and buffs essential to exploration and battle.

Immersive Physics

Use wind, fire, metal, and momentum to solve problems creatively. The physics system is a playground limited only by your imagination.
