Development Timeline

A decade of innovation, vision, and evolution.

2011 – Conception

Development began with a mission to rethink the Zelda formula, setting the foundation for a seamless open-world experience unlike anything seen before.

1986 Inspiration

The team revisited the original Legend of Zelda, drawing from its sense of freedom and curiosity to influence the new direction.

2015 – Delay for Innovation

Originally scheduled for 2015, the game was delayed to expand content and refine core mechanics, including physics and survival systems.

2017 – Dual Launch

The game launched in March 2017 for both Wii U and as a flagship title for the Nintendo Switch, showcasing a new era for the series in HD glory.
