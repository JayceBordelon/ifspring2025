The Legend Unfolds

A century of silence. A kingdom in ruins. A hero reborn.

Link Awakens

Set at the end of the Zelda timeline, Link awakens from a 100-year slumber with no memory of his past. He learns from King Rhoam that Calamity Ganon is sealed within Hyrule Castle — but growing stronger.

Reclaim the Divine Beasts

Link must regain control of four ancient Divine Beasts, each once piloted by a Champion. These towering machines hold the key to weakening Ganon’s grip on Hyrule.

Free the Spirits of the Champions

Inside the Beasts, Link confronts corrupted forms of Ganon. Defeating them frees the spirits of fallen Champions, restoring their strength to aid him.

Face Calamity Ganon

With the Beasts and the Master Sword, Link storms Hyrule Castle for a final showdown. Victory means salvation. Failure means ruin.

Memories of Zelda

Along the way, Link unlocks memories of Princess Zelda and their unbreakable bond — her wisdom, sacrifice, and hope are the light guiding him.
