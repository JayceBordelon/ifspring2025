The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch and Wii U consoles. Released on March 3, 2017, it marks a significant evolution in the Zelda series by introducing a vast open world and emphasizing player freedom.

Players control Link, who awakens from a century-long slumber to defeat Calamity Ganon and restore peace to the kingdom of Hyrule.
